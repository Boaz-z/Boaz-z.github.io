  • About Us

  • Welcome to GLTCHD, where creativity knows no bounds and innovation is our playground. Founded in 2025, GTCHD is an in the Netherlands based art collective that thrives on pushing the boundaries of conventional thinking. We are a team of artists, designers, and creators who believe in the power of the unexpected.


    At GLTCHD, we are dedicated to crafting experiences. Our projects span across various mediums, from digital art and viral internet stunts to unique product designs. Each creation is a testament to originality and our desire to make the world a more interesting place.


    Our work is driven by a simple philosophy:

    Why not?

    We embrace the absurd, the unconventional, and the extraordinary. Whether it's a pair of shoes made from clouds or an item that's completely lost its original purpose, we are constantly exploring new ways to engage and entertain.


    Join us on our journey as we continue to redefine the boundaries of art and creativity. At GLTCHD, we don't just think outside the box,

    we obliterate it.

  • Frequently Asked Questions

    Welcome to the GLTCHD FAQ. We’re a business with a slightly unconventional vibe. If you're wondering who we are, what we do, or how this all works; you're in the right place.








  • What is GLTCHD?

    GLTCHD is a creative studio and product company that designs unexpected, thought-provoking, and sometimes unconventional drops. We operate at the intersection of art, commerce, and culture. If you're looking for something ordinary, you're in the wrong place.




  • What kinds of things does GLTCHD release?

    We create limited-edition products, digital experiments, physical objects, and experiences. Some have utility, some are purely conceptual—but everything we release is intentional, collectible, and built to challenge assumptions.




  • How often do drops happen?

    There’s no fixed schedule. We launch drops when we believe they're ready. Some months are quiet; others move fast. The best way to stay informed is by joining our mailing list or following us on social media.




  • Are your products real?

    Yes. Everything we release is real. Even the ideas. Especially the ideas. If it's available for purchase, we guarantee fulfillment.




  • How do I know if a drop is live?

    We announce drops through email, our website, and on social platforms. If you’re subscribed and paying attention, you’ll know.




  • What’s your return and refund policy?

    All sales are final unless a product arrives damaged or defective. If there's a problem with your order, go to our contact page and contact us within 7 days of delivery and we’ll make it right.




  • Do you ship internationally?

    Yes, we ship globally whenever possible. Please note that international orders may be subject to duties and customs fees, which are the responsibility of the buyer.




  • I missed a drop, can I still get it?

    Most of our drops are limited edition and will not be restocked. Occasionally, we release a second run or archive piece, but once something is sold out, it's generally gone for good.




  • Can I collaborate with GLTCHD?

    We’re always open to conversations with artists, designers, and brands who align with our mission and aesthetic. If you have something in mind, contact us.




  • Are you hiring?

    We work with a small core team and a wide network of collaborators. If you’re interested in working with us, feel free to introduce yourself via our contact page. Include something real, not just a resume.




  • What does GLTCHD stand for?

    GLTCHD is a reflection of digital noise, signal disruption, and the creative potential in imperfection. If you see more in it, that’s exactly the point.
